You proceeded to head to a town across the Mountain you saw climbing your way up, it was filled with people going on to their activities.

There were some shops and everyone seemed busy. So you decided to put your cart in the middle of town where a couple of merchants were selling their merchandise.

By the end of the day, you gain a lot of gold and glee from happy customers who enjoyed a nice hot cup of tea. The Moon started to rise so you begin to put your equipment away when you receive your last customers for the day.
